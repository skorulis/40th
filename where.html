Sydney Park Pavilion

What's there

We have a hall and an outdoor terrace overlooking Alan Davidson Oval
The hall has bathrooms, a kitchen and fridges.
The pavilion is inside Sydney park which has plenty of places for children to explore

Public transport

The closest train station is St Peters.
Bus numbers 308, 348, 370 and 422 make stops near Sydney Park

Parking

The nearest available parking is in the general car park off Sydney Park Road and is highlighted in blue on the map.
The car park that is accessed from Euston Road (highlighted in yellow on the map below), may be closed at the time
Parking in Sydney park is free for 4 hours
